Karen Groener is an artist and designer whose desire was to steep her identity as an leather/fur expert in the sprit of New York, so she chose to locate her studio in West Village 30 years ago after she studied Fine Arts at Parsons. She runs a leather studio called Grown Beans with a exceptional team of artisans and craftsmen. Her workmanship and designs brought celebrity clients such as Missy Eliott and 50 Cent. She also enjoys teaching her skills and knowledge to students at Parsons and Fashion Institute of Technology.
